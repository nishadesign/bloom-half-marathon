## Brand Positioning

**Positioning Statement:**
For fitness enthusiasts who track their data but lack actionable insights, Bloom is the app that connects nutrition to performance and builds accountability through community. Unlike siloed tracking apps, we tell you exactly what works for YOUR body and keep you consistent through social support.

**Core Promise:**
Know what works. Track together. Perform better.

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **Cross-domain intelligence** - We connect nutrition → performance in ways single-purpose apps can't
2. **Certainty over confusion** - Clear, actionable answers instead of raw data dumps
3. **Social accountability built-in** - Not tracking alone, but with your crew

---

## Brand Personality

**The Smart Workout Partner**

We're the friend who happens to know a lot about fitness and nutrition - encouraging and knowledgeable, but never preachy or clinical. We speak like a supportive workout partner who's genuinely invested in your progress.

**Personality Attributes:**
- **Encouraging** - Celebrates wins, motivates through setbacks
- **Knowledgeable** - Gives smart recommendations backed by your data
- **Friendly** - Conversational, approachable, never robotic
- **Direct** - Clear and honest, no BS or fluff
- **Supportive** - Your ally, not your judge

---

## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### General Voice
**Conversational, encouraging, and smart without being technical**

**Good Examples:**
- "Nice! That pre-workout meal was perfectly timed. Your run should feel strong today 💪"
- "Heads up - you're 30g short on protein today and have a workout tomorrow. Let's fix that!"
- "You crushed that workout! Now fuel up within 2 hours for better recovery."

**Bad Examples:**
- "Analysis indicates 87% correlation coefficient between carbohydrate intake..." ❌ (too clinical)
- "You need to eat more protein immediately." ❌ (too harsh)
- "OMG you're doing amazing!!! 🎉🎉🎉" ❌ (too over-the-top)

### Emoji Usage
Use emojis sparingly.

## Visual Direction

### Brand Feel
**Clean, minimal, approachable**

Simple design creates simple interactions. Not clinical, not overly technical, not intimidating. Modern and warm, data-driven but human.

### Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Obsidian | `#1C1C1C` | Primary — text, dark surfaces, CTA backgrounds |
| Graphite | `#4A4A4A` | Secondary text, inactive states |
| Sand | `#C8B89A` | Accent — sparingly on highlights, active states |
| Linen | `#F0EBE3` | Surface — metric tiles, cards |
| Parchment | `#FAFAF8` | App background |

**Usage rule:** Sand is the only accent color. Use sparingly — highlights, active states. Everything else is Obsidian and Parchment. Restraint is key.

### Typography

| Role | Size | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display | 28px | 500 | Letter-spacing -0.5px, line-height 1.2 |
| Heading | 18–22px | 500 | Sentence case always |
| Body | 16px | 400 | Line-height 1.7 |
| Label | 11px | 500 | Uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em |
| Stat | 32px | 500 | Tabular figures |

**Rules:** Sentence case everywhere. Never title case, never all caps (except labels). Two weights only: 400 regular and 500 medium.

### Spacing System

Use multiples of 8px for consistent spacing:
- 8px - XS
- 16px - SM
- 24px - MD
- 32px - LG
- 48px - XL
- 64px - 2XL
